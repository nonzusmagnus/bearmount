Production notes

  • Both VO tracks are designed to run continuously under their scene's action — treat them as a bed, not sync'd to specific cuts, so the editor has room to play with rhythm in the montage.
  • The 0:15–0:16 pull-out is the hinge of the whole film — it should read as one unbroken camera move even if it's stitched from two separate takes/locations in post.
  • Confirm which Scene 1 open (bedroom alarm vs. park bench watch) fits the Airbnb/St Anne's Park schedule before the shoot day — both are scripted and ready to go.
  • French dialogue needs a native/fluent speaker on set for delivery timing, or a scratch VO reference track so lip timing holds in the edit.
  • Final product card (logo, name, tagline) to be confirmed and dropped over the 0:29–0:32 frame in post.
